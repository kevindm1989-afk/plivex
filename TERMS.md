# About Plivex

**Last updated:** 2026-05-10 (v1.2.0)

This file describes what Plivex is and is not, so anyone reading the source or reviewing the app understands what its design promises and what it does not.

## Plain language summary

Plivex is a free, personal note-taking app. It is not professional advice or evidence-management software. The technical properties described in this file — local-only storage, encryption, tamper-evident hash chain — are properties of the code as written. They are not legal claims.

## In detail

### 1. Nature of the software

Plivex is a personal note-taking application provided free of charge by an individual developer in their personal capacity. It is not a commercial product, not a professional service, and not affiliated with any organization, employer, or union.

### Auto-lock

The app locks itself automatically after a period of inactivity (default 15 minutes, user-configurable in Settings). After auto-lock, the master key is no longer in memory; you must re-enter your passphrase to access your entries again.

### 2. Not professional advice

Plivex is not legal advice, medical advice, financial advice, or advice of any other professional nature. It is a tool for personal documentation only. If you need professional advice about any matter you choose to document with this app, consult a qualified professional.

### 3. Not evidence-management software

Plivex is a personal notes app. It is not certified, audited, or designed for use as formal evidence-management software in any legal, regulatory, or institutional context. The "tamper-evident" feature describes a technical property of the data structure; it does not make entries automatically admissible in any proceeding. Whether and how any output of this app may be used in any proceeding is a matter for the user, their counsel, and the relevant tribunal.

### 4. Your responsibilities

By using this app, you accept that:

- You are solely responsible for backing up your data. The app has no backup service. If you do not export your data, it can be lost permanently.
- You are solely responsible for the accuracy of what you record. The app records what you type; it does not verify it.
- You are solely responsible for understanding any legal, professional, or regulatory rules that apply to records you create or keep.
- You are solely responsible for the security of the device on which you install the app, including your passphrase.
- If you forget your passphrase, your data cannot be recovered. There is no recovery mechanism.
